About Me

my Story

My journey into web development started in 2022. After spending over five years as a lab technician, multiple friends kept encouraging me to apply my talents to web development, and I never looked back. I love the challenge of creating unique user experiences while keeping accessibility as a top priority. My analytical skills from working in science combined with the creativity of web development gives me a unique skillset suited for any project.

In my spare time, you can find me on Twitch where I crochet stuffed animals (amigurumi) or learning to play sidescroller games. Currently, I'm playing Ori and the Will of the Wisps.
